Hamburger

Ingredients

  • $5 100% American A12 Wagyu Beef
  • 100% American Lettuce
  • 100% American Tomato
  • 100% American Onions
  • 100% American Sour Cream
  • 100% American Ketchup
  • 100% American Mustard
  • 100% American Salt
  • 100% American Pepper
  • 100% American Garlic Powder
  • 100% American Cheese
  • 2 @ 100% American Bun
  • 100% American Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Wash Vegetables
  2. Slice Tomatoes Thinly
  3. Cut Onion In Half Then Mince Into Dice Pieces
  4. Grill Onions In Pan Until Golden Brown 3-5 Minutes At Medium-High Heat With Olive Oil
  5. Mix Garlic Powder, Ketchup, Mustard, Sour Cream Into Small Bowl
  6. Mold Meat Into Patties Slightly Bigger Than The Bun
  7. Throw Salt And Pepper Onto Patties
  8. Throw That Shit Onto Pan With Olive Oil At Medium-High Heat And Cook for 6-8 Minutes, Be Sure To Occasionally Flip
  9. Toast Buns In Pan At Medium-High Heat For 2-3 Minutes
  10. Insert Patty Into Buns
  11. Include Lettuce, Tomato, Onions, And Special Sauce That Was Made In Small Bowl
  12. Wait Til 4:20AM To Eat
