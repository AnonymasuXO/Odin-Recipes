Instant Noodles

Ingredients

  • 100% American Instant Noodles Package
  • 100% American Water
  • 100% American Microwave

Directions

  1. Unwrap Instant Noodles
  2. Yeet Water Into Cup
  3. Toss Gently Into Microwave
  4. Heat In Microwave Until Lit (3 Mins)
  5. Do The Jerk For 2 Minutes Or Until Cooled Down Enough
  6. Backflip, Done.
